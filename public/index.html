Responsive domain name

What's this all about

We're currently obsessed with making everything responsive, but haven't we forgotten something? That's right, what about our URLs? Resize your browser now and see a responsive URL in action. But why stop there what about other things ourside our HTML canvas, our <title> tags and maybe even our favicons?

Examples

The URL you're on now is obviously just for demonstration purposes but here's a few real world examples you could use...

  • Blog post URLs could shrink to their IDs - yoururl.com/blog/post/example-slug-here could go to yoururl.com/blog/18
  • Dates are ripe for this sort of thing - yoururl.com/blog/archive/month/january/18/ could shrink down to yoururl.com/blog/01/18
  • Your team pages - yoururl.com/about-us/team/web/chris-rowe could be yoururl.com/about/chris
  • Your contact page? - yoururl.com/send-us-an-enquiry-email down to yoururl.com/contact
Think of the possibilities…
